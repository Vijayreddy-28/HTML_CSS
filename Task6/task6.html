Banglore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the technology hub of India. It is famous for its IT industry, startups, and modern lifestyle. The city blends urban development with greenery, featuring parks like Lalbagh and Cubbon Park. It has a pleasant climate and a vibrant culture with food, nightlife, and innovation.

Manglore

Mangalore (Mangaluru) is a coastal city known for its beautiful beaches, temples, and rich cultural heritage. It lies along the Arabian Sea and is famous for seafood cuisine and traditional architecture. The city has a calm, scenic vibe with a mix of modern life and coastal traditions.

Mysore

Mysore (Mysuru) is known as the “City of Palaces.” It is famous for its royal heritage, especially the Mysore Palace, and its connection to the Wodeyar dynasty. The city is clean, well-planned, and rich in culture, known for Dasara celebrations, silk, and sandalwood products.
